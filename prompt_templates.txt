=== REAL ESTATE AI PROMPT TEMPLATES ===
Built by 10th Dimension | Real Estate AI Automation Project

---

[1] PROPERTY LISTING DESCRIPTION
---------------------------------
Prompt:
You are a professional real estate copywriter in India. Write a compelling 150-200 word property listing description for the following:

Property Type: [3BHK / 2BHK / Villa / Plot]
Location: [Area, City]
Area: [sq ft]
Price: [INR]
Key Features: [list features]
Nearby: [schools, hospitals, IT parks]

Make it persuasive, highlight lifestyle benefits, and end with a call to action.

---

[2] CLIENT FOLLOW-UP EMAIL
--------------------------
Prompt:
Write a professional follow-up email to a property buyer named [Client Name] who visited [Property Name] on [Date].
The email should thank them, highlight 2-3 benefits of the property, and include a soft call-to-action.
Keep it under 150 words. Tone: warm and professional.

---

[3] WHATSAPP FOLLOW-UP MESSAGE
-------------------------------
Prompt:
Write a short WhatsApp follow-up message (max 60 words) for a real estate agent to send to a client after a site visit.
Property: [Property Name]
Client Name: [Name]
Make it friendly, non-pushy, and include one question to keep the conversation going.

---

[4] PROPERTY PRICING ANALYSIS
------------------------------
Prompt:
Analyze the fair market value of a property with these details:
Location: [Area, City]
Type: [Apartment/Villa/Plot]
Area: [sq ft]
Year Built: [Year]
Nearby landmarks: [list]
Compare with current market trends in that area and suggest a price range in INR.

---

[5] MARKETING SOCIAL MEDIA POST
--------------------------------
Prompt:
Create an engaging Instagram/Facebook post for a real estate listing:
Property: [Type + Location]
Highlights: [2-3 key features]
Price: [INR]
Contact: [Phone/Website]
Use emojis, keep it under 100 words, and add 5 relevant hashtags.

---

[6] PROPERTY INQUIRY CHATBOT REPLY
-----------------------------------
Prompt:
You are a real estate assistant chatbot. Reply to this customer inquiry:
"[Customer Message]"
Provide helpful information about the property, answer their question, and invite them to book a site visit.
Keep the reply under 100 words and friendly.
